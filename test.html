About Us

Innovatus Technologies is powered by seasoned tech professionals with experience dating back to 1999. We began in Bangalore, building software for the banking and hospitality sectors, and expanded to Pune in 2005 to explore new industries and establish our training division.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction has helped us grow across domains—earning trust from businesses across India and abroad. We deliver smart, scalable, and reliable software solutions at competitive rates, while remaining highly responsive and client-focused.
